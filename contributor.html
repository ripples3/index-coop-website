Welcome to a community of skilled, driven and visionary people from all around the world

Under one common goal - making crypto investing simple for everyone - we harmoniously work together to power Index Coop

Together, we govern, manage, steer and improve Index Coop. Our decentralized and democratic system ensures that there is little hierarchy or all-powerful leader, only contributors of different tiers. Ideas get proposed on our forum, and each community member can participate in governing Index Coop via Snapshot voting.

What’s it like to work at Index Coop?

It’s not your background or where you come from that matters. What matters is how you leverage your unique skillset to drive success for Index coop as a whole. We value a culture of inclusivity and growth; and equal opportunities are given to everyone who wants to join the Owls. By combining the strengths of our deep, diverse community, we are shaping a new, better future of finance.

Why join Index Coop?

Be on the forefront of the financial revolution with us
Help us build the products that make investing in the crypto space simple and accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world. With your help, we are breaking down the barriers and flaws of the existing financial system.

Earn rewards
Get compensated for impactful work you do. Compensations are decided on by working group leaders in a fair and transparent manner. At Index Coop, there are no pay gaps, ever.

Build your network
Connect and collaborate with other builders and users of the crypto space. Work closely with market leaders, innovators and long-time investors, both internally in Index Coop and externally.

Set your own schedule
Work as much as you want, from anywhere in the world. Amplify your impact by only picking up projects you are passionate about. Spend all your time at Index Coop, or split it between multiple DAOs.

How is Index Coop organized?

Contributors at Index Coop form working groups, where each member is empowered to contribute in high leverage ways. You can pick a field where you already excel, or challenge yourself by joining an area you’re less familiar with. No matter if you are a marketer, engineer, sales expert, strategic thinker, social media expert or simply someone who likes to challenge systems and concepts: There is a place for you at Index Coop.

Working groups - A detailed description

Product

This cross-functional working group balances customers' needs with Index Coop’s engineering capacity. Product leads are the ones who identify and launch new products that have an excellent market fit. 

Things that happen here:

Customer analysis

Cooperation with engineering to see what’s possible on the technical side

Cooperation with internal and external methodologists to take products from idea to investment vehicle

Monitoring performance of products

Ensuring that existing products are usable and being bought (i.e. by keeping an eye on on-chain liquidity and marketing efforts)

Identifying and leveraging potential of intrinsic and extrinsic productivity of funds held

Growth

Here, the holistic growth strategy of Index Coop is formed and executed.

This entails:

Identifying core values and distilling them into the right messages

Identifying and forming strategic relationships with partners across DeFi

Increasing exchange listings and wallet integrations

Strategy and execution of

Product Launch Marketing

Social Media Marketing

Widening the funnel and capturing new users to grow the market share of on-chain indexes

Monitoring awareness and acquisition and adjusting the overall strategy accordingly

Monitoring unincentivized supply growth and unincentivized AUM

Learn More
Business Development

This group supplements and expands the scope of responsibilities of the growth working group, by focusing on sustainable growth both externally and internally.

If you contribute in this working group, you work on

Forming relationships with investors

Developing connections across all functions in the DeFi ecosystem

Acting as the official DAO representative for external BD engagement

Developing a framework for managing interactions with ecosystem partners

Ensuring that in-bound and out-bound conversations are effectively managed

Ensuring that best-in class materials on key topics are maintained, regularly updated and available for easy access across the DAO

Treasury

The treasury working is responsible for preserving, growing and sustaining our treasury, and, by doing so, making the Index Coop sustainable for the long term. 

Here we

Develop structures for diversifying and managing the treasury

Manage the operations account for funding and budgeting

Grow links to other DAO treasuries, in order to open the path to potential IC product purchases and knowledge sharing

Ensure our flexibility to settle expenses in non-$INDEX terms, like $USDC or $ETH

Develop and specify incentive schemes (i.e. Liquidity Mining, Methodologies Incentives)

Deploy capital to productively and safely generate APY

Learn More
Analytics

The Coop relies on data in order to function effectively across numerous areas (product decisions, KPIs, treasury management, growth opportunities, liquidity management, and more). The analytics working group collects and processes this data.

Members here are

Supporting work team analysis and product methodologists

Building Coop-level and product-level KPI dashboards

Performing ad-hoc user and market analysis to determine opportunities

Injecting objectivity into governance discussions and voting

Supporting the growth working group (i.e. by monitoring impressions, new users, etc.)
